SRINIDHI'S FAVOURITE MOVIES

My Top 3 Favourite Movies

1.ANNIYAN

A terrific movie that highlights the unimaginable amount of corruption that happens in the country
and the need to change from within ourselves in order to reform the society.
My childhood favourite.

2.ABHIYUM NAANUM

A beautiful movie that showcases the immense love and over-protectiveness a father shows towards his daughter.
What I love about the movie is the way it brings about all the intricate emotions
that are experienced by the parents during the growth of their child from a toddler to a mature adult.
And not to forget the sweet ending where sentiments run high not only in the screen but also in our homes.

3.NANBAN

An enlightening movie,especially for college students,that deals with the cons of the current education system.
Through the protoganist,who studies only for his own happiness and not for a job or money or to satisfy his parents,
the movie warns us not to become victims of the ranking system and the rat-race of today's competitive world.
It also wonderfully displays the purity of friendship.
Everytime I watch this movie,my body gets filled with a surge of motivation and a thirst for learning more.
Overall I love this movie.
